Banana Fritters Recipe

Everybody loves my banana fritters and everybody always wants me to make fritters for them.

Prep and Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 bananas
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of nutmeg
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1/2 a cup of milk
  • 1 cup of flour

Directions:

  1. Peel and mash the bananas. Add sugar, nutmeg and one beaten egg. Add milk and flour.
  2. Optional: sprinkle with white sugar.
Serves 10
